PREPARING GRAINS

STEEP YOUR GRAINS

ADD MALT EXTRACTS AND HOPS

BOIL MORE

YOU NOW HAVE THE WORT (SUGAR WATER)

TIME TO FERMENT

Add water, yeast, and air lock. Be sure to sanitize EVERYTHING that touches the brew. Store in a dark place for at least 2 weeks.

BOTTLING DAY

Sanitize bottles, use priming sugar, and transfer beer. Let beer sit again for a few weeks.
